The West Linn Community Chorus is a 501 (c)(3) non-profit organization dependent on the generosity of our community. We are funded through grants, ticket sales, fundraisers, membership fees and private donations. Support us through our Friends of the Chorus program or by shopping through our Amazon Smile or Sheet Music Plus links.

Friends of the Chorus

Donations to our Friends of the Chorus program enable us to pay our professional musicians, rent rehearsal and performance spaces, promote our events, and provide music to all of our members.

Donation Levels

Maestro $1,000 and up 8 complimentary tickets and program acknowledgement.
Virtuoso $500 – $999 6 complimentary tickets and program acknowledgement.
Prodigy $200 – $499 5 complimentary tickets and program acknowledgement.
Whole Note $100-$199 4 complimentary tickets and program acknowledgement.
Half Note $50-$99 2 complimentary tickets and program acknowledgement.
Quarter Note $25-$49 Program acknowledgement.

Kindly mail donations to: West Linn Community Chorus, PO Box 187, West Linn, OR 97068

Please write “Friends of the Chorus” in the memo line.

AmazonSmile

Support the chorus while you shop. Simply login through the AmazonSmile portal and designate the West Linn Community Chorus as your charity and Amazon will donate 0.5% of the price of your eligible purchases to West Linn Community Chorus.

Simply follow this link to AmazonSmile and login to your Amazon account as normal.
